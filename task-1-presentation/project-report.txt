I created this infographic using *Canva*, which is a popular online design tool for making presentations, posters, and infographics. Canva provides many templates, icons, and design elements that help in creating a professional and visually appealing slide easily. I selected a dark tech-themed background with blue and purple gradients to give the infographic a modern digital look.

My design shows the concept of *Digital Literacy for Students* and covers five important areas: what digital literacy is, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. Each section contains five key points and is supported by relevant icons such as laptop, shield, tools, profile, and email to make the information easy to understand and visually attractive.

One thing I found interesting while making this infographic was experimenting with colors, layouts, and icons to make the design look techy and organized at the same time. The difficult part was arranging all five sections neatly in a single slide without making it look crowded, but Canva’s grid and alignment tools helped me manage the layout properly.
