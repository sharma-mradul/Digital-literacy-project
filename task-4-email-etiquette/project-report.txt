One situation where poor digital communication caused a problem is when a student sent an informal and rude email to a professor asking for assignment marks. The email had no subject, no greeting, and used disrespectful language. This created a negative impression and the professor ignored the request. Poor digital communication like this can damage a student’s reputation and affect academic relationships. In such a situation, the student should have written a professional email with a clear subject line, polite greeting, and respectful tone. Proper email etiquette and professional communication help in building trust and maintaining good relationships with teachers and organizations. Therefore, students must always communicate responsibly and professionally in digital platforms.
