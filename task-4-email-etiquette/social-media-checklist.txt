# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ Do's

1. Think before posting any content online.
2. Maintain a professional and respectful tone.
3. Protect your personal information and privacy.
4. Share educational and useful content.
5. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
6. Respect others' opinions and communicate politely.

## ❌ Don'ts

1. Do not post offensive or abusive content.
2. Do not share fake news or unverified information.
3. Do not upload private photos or personal data publicly.
4. Do not argue or fight in comment sections.
5. Do not misuse social media during classes or academic time.
6. Do not post inappropriate content that can harm your career.

## Purpose

This checklist helps college students use social media responsibly, maintain a professional online presence, and avoid digital communication mistakes.
