# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist for College Students (India)

## 🔐 Cyber Safety Tips

1. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone.
2. Always check UPI payment requests carefully before approving them.
3. Do not click on unknown links received through SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for all social media and banking accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts.
6. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking or payments.
7. Verify job offers, internship emails, and payment requests before responding.
8. Install apps only from official sources like Google Play Store or App Store.
9. Regularly update your phone and apps to prevent security vulnerabilities.
10. Log out from shared or public computers after use.

## 💰 UPI & Financial Safety (Important)

- Never approve a UPI request to receive money (UPI requests are for sending money).
- Do not install screen-sharing apps like AnyDesk or TeamViewer when asked by unknown callers.

## 🚨 Report Cybercrime

If you face any cyber fraud:

- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
- Report immediately to stop further financial loss.

## 📌 Purpose

This checklist helps college students stay aware of online threats and protect their digital identity and financial information.
