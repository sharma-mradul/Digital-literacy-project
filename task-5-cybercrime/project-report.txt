While researching cybercrime and UPI fraud, I was most surprised by how quickly scammers can steal money using simple tricks like fake payment requests and OTP fraud. I learned that many victims lose their money within minutes because they do not understand how UPI requests work. It was also surprising to see how students and educated people can become victims due to lack of awareness and panic during calls from fake customer care agents. After researching this topic, one habit I will personally change is that I will always verify any payment request or unknown link before clicking or approving it. I will never share OTP or UPI PIN with anyone and will immediately report suspicious activity to the National Cyber Crime Portal or helpline 1930. This awareness will help me stay safe online and avoid cyber fraud.
